Providence, RI State Capital

  1. City's population

    As of July 1, 2024 the population estimate is 194,706.

  2. Year city was incorporated

    Providence was officially incorporated as a city in 1831 (ratified by residents) and officially chartered in 1832. Founded by Roger Williams in 1636, the town transitioned from a town meeting form of government to a city, following a population increase and the need for a stronger municipal structure.

  3. Region located

    Providence is located in the northeastern part of Rhode Island, situated at the head of Narragansett Bay in Providence County.

  4. City Hall

  • Classification of the city

    Providence is considered a distinct, densely populated urban city, often described as having an urban-suburban mix.

  • Average income level compared to rest of state

    State average: $87,796.

    City average: $68,119.
