Warwick

City's population

  1. As of July 1, 2024 the population estimate is 83,554.

    2. Year city was incorporated

  2. Warwick, Rhode Island, was officially incorporated as a city on April 21, 1931. Founded in 1642 as the settlement of Shawomet, it was previously incorporated as a town in 1647.

    3. Region located

  3. Warwick is located in the West Bay region of central Rhode Island in Kent County. Situated roughly 12 miles south of Providence along the western shore of Narragansett Bay, it serves as a central hub known for its extensive coastline, T.F. Green Airport, and suburban residential areas.

Classification of the city

  • Warwick, Rhode Island, is primarily a dense suburban city with a population of over 80,000. It acts as a suburban hub for nearby Providence while offering coastal living, featuring numerous restaurants, shops, and parks. It is frequently described as having a "suburban-urban mix" due to its developed, car-dependent nature.
